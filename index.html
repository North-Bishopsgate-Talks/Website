North Bishopsgate Talks

Sceptics, enquirers and Christians alike, are all warmly invited to join us each Thursday for lunch and a short bible talk. Sandwiches are served from 1pm, the talk starts at 1:10pm and lasts around 20 minutes followed by a chance to ask questions. We finish by 1:45pm.

Upcoming talks: Every Thursday, 12th Sep — 5th Dec 2019

Hope fulfilled: John 5-10

We live in an era of unprecedented wealth, information and connectedness. Yet at the same time we also live in a time of deepening dissatisfaction. Many in our society long for a sense of meaning, of freedom, of fulfilment, of rest from the treadmill. In John chapters 5-10, Jesus shows by both his actions and teachings, how he fulfilled all the hopes of the people of his own day and he invites us today to know true fulfilment in himself. The sad reality is that many in Jesus day were so caught up in their own way of doing things that they missed out on what he was offering. Carve out the time to join us at W M Barker's this term as we consider the breadth of Jesus’ claims afresh.
