Sufficient in Christ: Paul's letter to the Colossians

Christians often feel we are missing out on true spirituality. We are tempted by offers of special methods or spiritual experiences held out by so-called spiritual teachers. These teachers guarantee greater devotion to God and more success against sin through their methods and experiences. The Colossian church at the time of Paul was facing the same temptation as ourselves. Paul in his letter sets forth the sufficiency of Christ and urges the believers to hold on to him and to resist being taken captive by false teaching. Please do join us as we go through this Christ-filled letter together!
