North Bishopsgate Talks

Sceptics, enquirers and Christians alike, are all warmly invited to join us each Thursday for lunch and a short bible talk. Sandwiches are served from 1pm, the talk starts at 1:10pm and lasts around 20 minutes followed by a chance to ask questions. We finish by 1:45pm.

Venue

(37 Brushfield Street, London, E1 6AA)

Dates

Every Thursday lunchtime in term time, starting on 10th January and running until 3rd April next term.

Timings

Sandwiches will be available from 1pm. The talk will start at 1:10pm and last for around 20-25 minutes. There will then be time for people to ask questions and discuss the talk if they have time to stick around. We will finish formally at 1:45pm sharp.


Upcoming talks: Every Thursday, 10th Jan — 3rd Apr 2019

John 1-4: The Hope of Glory — How Jesus calls us to a deeper wonder

As humanity we love to celebrate and to applaud anything which we consider glorious or wonderful. It may be it a work of art, a performance of music, or a great mind. It is a powerful instinct: energising when we worship something that is good, but destructive when we worship something unworthy. Join us as we explore the early chapters of John’s gospel where Jesus claims to lead us into a deeper glory. There will be a 20 minute talk for Christians, cynics and enquirers alike followed by an opportunity to ask questions & discuss.

About

He left finance in 2012 and was ordained as a vicar in the Church of England in 2014.
