THE HOPE OF GLORY — John 1-4
How Jesus calls us to a deeper wonder

As humanity we love to celebrate and to applaud anything which we consider glorious or wonderful. It may be it a work of art, a performance of music, or a great mind. It is a powerful instinct: energising when we worship something that is good, but destructive when we worship something unworthy. Join us as we explore the early chapters of John’s gospel where Jesus claims to lead us into a deeper glory. There will be a 20 minute talk for Christians, cynics and enquirers alike followed by an opportunity to ask questions & discuss.
